# About Glean

> Turn your scroll habit into action

Saving stuff online is free, doing anything with it is not. Most people end up with a graveyard of tweets starred, videos saved, and articles in Readwise or Pocket, none of which translates into action. Glean re-threads that flow: share any piece of content from iOS, Chrome, or the web dashboard, and the AI extracts the core idea into a structured todo with source context attached. You end up with a todo list shaped by your actual interests instead of a bookmark pile you never revisit.
