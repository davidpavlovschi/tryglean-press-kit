# Glean roadmap
Glean is a capture app for iOS, Chrome, and web. Share any tweet, YouTube video, article, or screenshot and Glean's AI extracts the key ideas into a structured, actionable todo with full context. Stop bookmarking, start doing.
## Where we are
Glean is the capture app for people who save 200 tweets a week and act on zero of them. Available on iOS, Chrome, and web, it takes any piece of content (tweet, YouTube video, article, screenshot) and runs it through an AI layer that extracts the key ideas, preserves the source context, and generates a structured todo. The todo lives in your Glean inbox with full provenance, so you can pick it up three weeks later and remember exactly what you wanted to do. It sits between a read-it-later tool and a task manager.
