# Topics covered by Glean

Glean is a multi-platform capture tool that turns your scroll habit into real output. Share a tweet, a YouTube video, an article, or a screenshot from the iOS share sheet, the Chrome extension, or the web dashboard. The AI pulls the key ideas, preserves the source context, and creates a structured todo you can actually act on. A Readwise-meets-Todoist for the attention economy.
