# Glean — Press Kit
> Turn your scroll habit into action
Official press kit for **[Glean](https://tryglean.app)**. This repo is a permanent, linkable brand reference for journalists, directories, and integrations.
## About
Glean is the capture app for people who save 200 tweets a week and act on zero of them. Available on iOS, Chrome, and web, it takes any piece of content (tweet, YouTube video, article, screenshot) and runs it through an AI layer that extracts the key ideas, preserves the source context, and generates a structured todo. The todo lives in your Glean inbox with full provenance, so you can pick it up three weeks later and remember exactly what you wanted to do. It sits between a read-it-later tool and a task manager.

## Long-form description
Saving stuff online is free, doing anything with it is not. Most people end up with a graveyard of tweets starred, videos saved, and articles in Readwise or Pocket, none of which translates into action. Glean re-threads that flow: share any piece of content from iOS, Chrome, or the web dashboard, and the AI extracts the core idea into a structured todo with source context attached. You end up with a todo list shaped by your actual interests instead of a bookmark pile you never revisit.
